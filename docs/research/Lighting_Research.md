# Lighting Research

## Why lighting was isolated

Early camera conclusions used lighting and aesthetic phrases in the baseline. Clean re-testing showed that these phrases can change framing, subject scale, pose, scene, and visible equipment, so they require their own contamination audit.

## `soft lighting`

### Alone

Observed face-to-upper-body near views, larger subjects, simplified backgrounds, and a soft portrait cluster.

### With wide shot

- `wide shot + soft lighting`: distant and near results mixed; large luminous backgrounds increased.
- `soft lighting + wide shot`: mostly near portraits; later wide shot did not restore the distance consistently.

### With full body

`full body + soft lighting` produced full body 6/6 and sitting/bent-knee/cross-legged compression 6/6. The current interpretation is not “soft lighting requests upper body,” but “soft lighting prefers large portrait scale.” Full visibility plus large subject is solved by body compression. Reversing phrase order gave similar behavior, so semantic constraint interaction exceeded simple ordering.

## `studio lighting`

`wide shot + studio lighting` retained a small/full subject and expanded the scene into spotlights, visible fixtures, dark stage/background, or a studio. It used camera-provided spatial budget as scene structure instead of collapsing the shot.

## Quality/aesthetic isolation

| Trial | Result |
|---|---|
| Clean full body | 6/6 standing. |
| `4k, high resolution, ultra-detailed` | 6/6 standing. |
| `very aesthetic` | 5/6 standing, 1/6 floor. |
| `masterpiece + best quality` | 5/6 floor pose. |
| `masterpiece` at front | 4/6 floor, 2/6 standing. |
| `masterpiece` at end | 3/6 floor, 3/6 standing. |
| `best quality` alone | 4/6 standing, 2/6 floor. |
| `masterpiece + best quality` moved to end | Floor poses decreased relative to front placement. |

Interpretation: `masterpiece` was the main aesthetic composition/pose cause, `best quality` a weaker amplifier, and `very aesthetic` weaker again. `4k/high resolution/ultra-detailed` was comparatively safe in scope. Front aesthetic phrases may set the initial cluster; later phrases act more as corrections, though position does not erase their semantic effects.

## Current audit matrix

For each lighting phrase, record:

- framing change;
- subject-scale change;
- scene generation;
- visible light source/equipment;
- pose change;
- outfit/face leakage;
- cooperation/suppression by camera and background;
- activation variance and model dependence.

Open representatives: dramatic lighting, rim lighting/rim light, backlighting/backlight, cinematic lighting, volumetric lighting, and colored lighting.
